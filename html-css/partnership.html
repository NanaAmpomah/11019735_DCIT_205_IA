Overview

Welcome to the Industrial Partnerships page of the Computer Science at the University of Ghana. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the classroom, and we take pride in fostering dynamic collaborations with industry leaders. These partnerships play a pivotal role in shaping the educational experience of our students and driving cutting-edge research initiatives.

Our Industry Partners

Discover the power of collaboration by exploring our network of esteemed industry partners. These partnerships span various sectors, offering students and faculty invaluable opportunities to engage with real-world challenges and innovations. Some of our partners include:

  • AFOS Foundation for Entrepreneurial Development Cooperation.
  • Brandenburg University of Technology.
  • Deutsche Welle Akademie.
  • Duale Hochschule Baden-Wurtteemberg.
  • Freie Universitat Berlin.
Internship Programs

Our students gain hands-on experience through internships with leading companies. Explore the pathways that connect classroom learning to professional practice and hear success stories from students who have thrived in these immersive experiences.
